About

As a freshly-minted full stack developer, I love planning and wireframing an application, architecting the data and building out the back end, then visualizing it for the user. In my bootcamp (Coding Dojo) I found that I loved working with a team to bring a vision to life. I think I enjoy talking through a project with my team so much because I feel confident articulating complex ideas. Most of that confidence is due to my writing background.

Just before COVID I was in New York City finishing my MFA program in fiction writing. My plan during lockdown was to put together a strong body of work for PhD programs in literature, but I found my interest in academia waning and my curiosity about software development increasing. I dabbled in Python, then started to find that, at the most basic level, I love building things and solving practical problems, even if it’s just a bite-sized experimental application. After a period of self-directed study, I enrolled in the three-stack program at Coding Dojo, and I haven’t looked back since.

Some of my long-term interests include education, urban planning, and clean energy. In my free time, I wish I had time to make the next Stardew Valley, but I need to finish writing this novel…
